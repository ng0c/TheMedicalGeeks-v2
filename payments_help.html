Payments

We accept all major credit/debit cards, Paypal. and Apple payments. We also accept Bitcoin payments too!

Once your personal statement has been sent, and is being edited, we cannot offer a refund on the personal statement service. If you want to cancel the payment, send a email ASAP after your payment, and hopefully we can cancel the service before our team gets editing!

Unfortunantly because our guides are digital, we cannot reclaim the product back if a refund is claimed. Therefore, we cannot accept refunds for ANY of our guides. If you do feel like the product is completely unsatisfactory, please give us a reason and contact us at themedicalgeeks@gmail.com

If you have received a discount code because of a bursary or a special offer for you and its not working, please send an email ASAP (themedicalgeeks@gmail.com) and we'll get back to you with a new code and the product sent directly to you. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause!
