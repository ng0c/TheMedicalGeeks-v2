COVID-19 And UCAT Latest Updates

Latest Update - 01/06/2020

It's important to keep up to date with the UCAT. On this page, we will post the latest information here, but always check the official UCAT website here.It's constantly changing every week, so check the UCAT website for the latest information.

Will The UCAT Still Occur?

The UCAT exam will still form an important part of the application process into medicine. Although the setting maybe different compared to previous years, scores WILL STILL BE USED in the application process to form interview places.

Will Scoring Be Different For The UCAT?

As it stands, there will be now difference in how the scoring system will be done.

Although its potentially going to be a strange year for scores. Because the UCAT tests are going to be sat in different places as explained below, there is the potential for there to be a shift in the scores. Whether that means there will be a mean average increase/decrease, we will have to wait and see.

Quick note: Obviously this is relative. So scoring 500 this year may be considered a good score, whereas in previous years it would be considered low.

Should You Register For The UCAT Exam?

Yes! You should still register here to do your exam. If the situation becomes incredibly bad, Pearson will contact you regarding what to do next.





What Does TheMedicalGeeks Think?

These are difficult times, and this year is going to be difficult for everyone. Its important to note that everyone is in the same position as you.

There should be no difference in the preparation for the UCAT exam compared to previous years. The only difference is that you may have to keep in touch with regular updates either on this page, or on UCAT's Official Website.

From everyone at TheMedicalGeeks, keep safe and study hard, and good luck.
