Frequently Asked Questions

Interview Service

Make sure you check your Spam folder and your All Inbox too! If you still can't find it, contact us themedicalgeeks@gmail.com and we'll send a copy to you directly.

For your mock interview service, you will always have medical students. The majority of the interviews will be done by the co-founders Ryan and Sam, however there may be the occasion where our Product Manager Makeen will step in!

We understand that some applicants live in really rural areas and the internet can be bad. If you arrive on time to the appointment allocated to you, and the internet is affecting the quality of the interview, we may offer a refund to you. We advise that you try and find locations with stable internet access. Simple tricks such as doing the mock interview closer to your WiFi router will improve connection, or arranging an interview during your school time, so you will have a stable connection at school. Public libraries are also very good locations to do interviews too.

From our experience, we recommend doing the 1st mock interview session 2 weeks away from your interview. Then 1 week away from your interview, have the 2nd session. This allows you to have time to change and make any corrections from the 1st practice session and theres enough time from the 2nd session to make sure you're ready for the big day.

Each session is 1 hour long. We cover a range of topics roughly equating 5 stations and some feedback is given during that 1 hour.

If theres some specific questions/areas you would like to work on, let us know prior to the interview. We will then try to work on giving you a more personalised experience. However the stations we do provide do cover the majority of MMI stations / traditional questions that may arise from medical schools.

During each session, the interviews will be constantly making notes. In these notes, we will create an electric document from each interviewer for you, so you can see what score you would have received for each station and some feedback from each station too.

Interviews can be subjective. We try and provide a realistic score and feedback based off our experience from the medical interviews and interviewers themselves. Scoring low does not mean you will do bad on the day. If you did score poor from us, use the feedback from us and it will point you in the right direction, so come interview day, you're ready to nail it!

Remember the interviewers have lives too and are at medical school so please bear that in mind and try not to be late! The session is booked for an hour from the time allocated so if you are late, we will not be running overtime for you.

We offer a 12 hour cancellation window prior to your mock interview. If you want to move your interview day to another date, please try and do it 12 hours prior to the interview session so we can arrange another date for you. We cannot arrange another session if its sooner than 12 hours. If you feel after paying that you dont want the mock interview anymore, contact us at themedicalgeeks@gmail.com and we'll offer a refund as long as its before 12 hours of the allocated mock interview.
