Our Aim

TheMedicalGeeks started as a small idea in a lecture hall whilst at university. TheMedicalGeeks founders (Sam and Ryan) were both talking about how expensive the application process of getting into medical school can be. Looking at peers who were planning on going into different professions, they didn't experience anything close to what aspiring medics have to endure. We wanted to create a friendly, cheap and personalised service that focuses on ensuring everyone has a great application to get into medical school. So many applicants are turned down in this competitive environment despite being more than capable of being an excellent medical student.

Why TMG

High Quality

We provide the highest quality of service there is. Every customer is of our highest priority and each application is tailored to your needs.

Give Back

The staff that are helping you have all been in your shoes. We know what it's like and how hard it can be. This also means we KNOW what YOU need. We want to give back to the aspiring medical community, so that everyone wanting to get into medicine has a great chance of becoming a doctor.

Affordable

The medical application process is already expensive. We saw many companies charging huge amounts for a less personalised service. At TheMedicalGeeks, delivering a higher quality standard at a cheaper price is our first concern.
