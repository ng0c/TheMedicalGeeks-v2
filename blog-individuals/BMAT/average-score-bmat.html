Where To Apply With An Average BMAT Score?

13/06/20

What is considered to be an average BMAT score? What universities ask for an average BMAT score? How do universities use average BMAT scores? How important is an average BMAT score when applying to medical school? What BMAT score do I need if I am applying to Brighton & Sussex Medical School or University College London (UCL)?

Introduction

To start with, at Brighton & Sussex, the personal statement is not used here. This diverts the attention of the university to other aspects of the application such as BMAT.

Lancaster University

There is no clear cut-off score for the BMAT here. It is basically just one aspect of the application that is used alongside others, such as GCSEs, predicted grades, personal statement and reference.

It is worth mentioning that you are likely to have to discuss your essay during your interview. Also, there is no section that is more important than others to Lancastre.

It seems that all sections are considered. For the sake of performing well if asked about your essay, we recommend you write as much as you can remember of that essay straight after you do the BMAT. That way you can remind yourself of what you wrote before your interview date.
